Community resources
===================

The `Open Microscopy Environment`_ provides a number of resources for
both user and developer communities to assist in use and
development of our software.  Contributions through the forums are always welcome.

.. _community/resources/web:

Web
---

The main OME website is at `<http://www.openmicroscopy.org/site>`_. Bio-Formats
is at `<http://www.openmicroscopy.org/site/products/bio-formats>`_.
